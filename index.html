Visit Bihar

From its bustling cities to tranquil wilderness, Bihar has something for everyone. Visit this culturally rich and diverse state of India.

Top three places to visit in Bihar

Gaya

Gaya is one of the best tourist places in Bihar that amazes visitors with its religious essence.

Nalanda

The main tourist attractions of Nalanda are Silao, Nalanda Multimedia Museum, Rajgir Dance Festival held in October, Surajpur Baragaon, etc.

Kanwar Lake Bird Sacntuary

Home to the largest freshwater Oxbow lake in Asia, Kanwar Lake Bird Sanctuary is a breeding spot for 60 migratory bird species
